About Me

Motohiko Makino is a software engineer with a natural knack for combining technical know-how with creative elements. He has 15 years of demonstrated experience working with IC test chip and modeling technology. He is well-versed in C/C++, Visual Basic, Perl, Ruby, and SQL. He uses his skills and creativity by playing a key role in providing solutions for integrating IC design, testing, and analysis. He is constantly expanding his knowledge and experience by keeping abreast of the evolving trends, techniques, and technologies to streamline efficiency. He is currently a part time student studying Full Stack Web Development. When he is not attached to a computer, he repeatedly plays the same old 80s rock tunes with his guitar, reads Russian novels from 19th century, and skiing on mountains with his son.
